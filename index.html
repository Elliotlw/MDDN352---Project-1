TRAIL PHOTOGRAPHY

WHAT IS TRAIL PHOTOGRAPHY?

It's always a struggle to find a trail that is aetheically pleasing for photography without going to the location before hand. Why not discover the beauty of trail around your area without even leaving the house? With Trail Photography view hundreds of photo's taken by user's of the app to find the perfect location for your photoshoot!


<

DISCOVER TRAILS

As well as discovering great photography locations the app is also useful for finding the perfect trail for your needs. The app indicates what activities can be carried out on each trail, whether it be walking/ running, cycling, dog walking or horse riding the app will let you know!


<

SUBMIT YOUR PHOTOGRAPHY

If you have amazing photos of a trail near you, you can submit them to the app for the world to view. After a quick sign up proccess the user can submit a photo within the app, it will then be approved by the development team, if it passes approval then it will be released to the world.


<

DIRECTIONS

If you find a trail that is good for your purposes then you can click the "directions" and it will transfer you to google maps to direct you to the starting location of the trail.


<

POINTS OF INTEREST

Once a trail is selected the user can scroll to the botttom of the page to open and view the points of interest map, this provides an overview of the trail pinpointing all the point of interest on the trails. This includes lookouts, monuments, river mouths or any point that is interesting for photography.
When submitting photos to the app the user can select if this photo is on a point of interest, enabling other users to view that photo on the point of interest by clicking on it


<

AVAILABILITY

This app is designed as a cross platform experience, opening accessability to people with all types of mobile devices as well as desktops and web platforms. The end goal is to provide a user friendly experience that provides all neccessary information for photographers as well as general trail goers.

Trail Photography will be availably on both iOs and Android
